## SEO & GEO

Covers keyword research, technical SEO, link building, AI search / GEO, and rank tracking. Tools marked \* require a paid subscription for full access.

**[Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com)** \* — All-in-one SEO platform for backlink analysis, keyword research, site audits, and competitor intelligence. Also check the [Ahrefs Blog](https://ahrefs.com/blog/) and [Ahrefs Academy](https://academy.ahrefs.com/) (free).

**[Semrush](https://semrush.com)** \* — Comprehensive SEO and competitive research suite covering keywords, backlinks, PPC, and content marketing. Free tier available; [Semrush Academy](https://www.semrush.com/academy/) courses are free.

**[Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)** — Monitor your site's presence in Google Search results, track clicks and impressions, and fix indexing issues. Free.

**[Screaming Frog SEO Spider](https://www.screamingfrog.co.uk/seo-spider/)** — Desktop crawler that audits websites for broken links, redirects, duplicate content, and on-page SEO issues. Free up to 500 URLs.

**[Moz Pro](https://moz.com/products/pro)** \* — SEO toolset with domain authority metrics, keyword explorer, rank tracking, and site crawl. Freemium.

**[Surfer SEO](https://surferseo.com)** \* — Content optimization tool that uses NLP to help you write pages that rank by analyzing top-ranking competitors.

**[Google Keyword Planner](https://ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner/)** — Research keywords and get search volume estimates directly from Google's advertising data. Free with a Google Ads account.

**[AnswerThePublic](https://answerthepublic.com)** — Visualizes search questions and phrases people type around any keyword, surfacing content opportunities. Freemium.

**[Exploding Topics](https://explodingtopics.com)** — Surfaces fast-growing topics and keywords before they peak, useful for staying ahead of search trends. Freemium.

**[AlsoAsked](https://alsoasked.com)** — Maps the "People Also Asked" question trees from Google SERPs to reveal related search intent at scale. Freemium.

**[Perplexity](https://perplexity.ai)** — AI-powered search engine that cites sources; use it to test how your brand appears in AI-generated answers (GEO testing). Freemium.

**[Google AI Overviews — Search Central documentation](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/ai-overviews)** — Official Google guidance on how pages are selected for AI Overviews and how to optimize for generative search. Free.

**[SEOFOMO by Aleyda Solis](https://www.seofomo.co/)** — Weekly newsletter covering SEO news, tools, and strategies including AI search developments. Free.

**[Search Engine Land](https://searchengineland.com/)** — Daily SEO and SEM news covering algorithm updates, industry research, and tactical guides. Free.

**[AdEspresso by Hootsuite](https://adespresso.com)** \* — Facebook and Instagram ad management and A/B testing platform with simplified campaign creation. Paid.

**[Optmyzr](https://optmyzr.com)** \* — PPC management platform that automates bid adjustments, budget pacing, and reporting for Google and Microsoft Ads. Paid.

**[Search Ads 360](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/search-ads-360/)** \* — Google's enterprise search campaign management platform for cross-engine bid strategy and reporting. Paid (enterprise).

**[Google Marketing Platform](https://marketingplatform.google.com/)** — Suite of Google's advertising and analytics products including Display & Video 360 and Campaign Manager 360. Freemium to paid.
