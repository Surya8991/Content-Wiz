============================================================
META / FACEBOOK ADS COPY PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Create Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ad copy for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], for the campaign targeting "[CAMPAIGN TOPIC / OFFER]". Generate 3 ad variations. For each variation provide: primary text (max 125 characters for feed preview, up to 300 for full), a headline (max 40 characters), a description (max 30 characters), and a CTA button label from Meta's options (Learn More / Sign Up / Download / Get Quote / Contact Us / Apply Now). Copy should be written in a direct, benefit-led tone targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Open with a hook. Do not use em dashes.

Landing Page URL: [INSERT URL]
Ad Placement: [e.g. Facebook Feed / Instagram Feed / Stories / Reels]
Campaign Goal: [e.g. Awareness / Lead gen / Traffic / Conversions]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Ad Variation | Primary Text | Headline | Description | CTA Button | Placement

Save to output/Meta_Facebook_Ads/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Primary text: max 125 characters for preview (up to 300 full)
- Headline: max 40 characters
- Description: max 30 characters
- CTA must be one of Meta's native options
- First line of primary text must be a hook
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- If any statistic appears in the copy, name its source, organization, and year
- 3 ad variations per request
- Vary the exact phrasing of the hook line across variations and across requests, do not reuse the same hook in the same batch. Examples of the pattern (write a new one each time, do not copy these verbatim): "Still tracking completion, not results?", "Your next cohort starts in weeks - is your team ready?", "The training gap nobody budgets for."
- The identical hook line reused across multiple ad variations or requests in the same batch - if you are generating more than one set of ads in a session, no two variations may share a hook line
- Output saved to output/Meta_Facebook_Ads/
