============================================================
BLOG SUGGESTION PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Generate 10 blog topic ideas for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. The topics should be SEO-friendly, relevant to the niche "[NICHE/TOPIC AREA]", and suitable for publication on [PLATFORM e.g. Medium / WordPress / brand blog]. For each idea, provide a suggested title, a 1–3 sentence description stating the reader problem and specific angle the piece would take, and the primary target keyword. Do not use em dashes. Topics should range in difficulty and format (listicles, how-tos, guides, thought leadership).
Voice/Tone: [formal B2B / conversational B2B / first-person / brand account — default: professional B2B]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Suggested Title | Angle / Description | Primary Keyword | Format Type

Field encoding: wrap any field containing commas or line breaks in double quotes.
Angle/Description: 1–3 sentences, states the reader problem and the specific angle in concrete terms (e.g. "Most L&D managers track completion, not behavior change. This post shows a 3-step audit to close that gap.").
Suggested Title must be a complete headline, not a topic label.

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- 10 topic ideas per request
- Mix of formats: listicles, how-tos, ultimate guides, thought leadership, case studies
- Each title should be SEO-friendly with a natural keyword
- Angle / Description must be at least 1 full sentence (not a fragment or keyword string) stating the specific angle the piece would take
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- SELF-CHECK: Each Angle/Description must be 1–3 sentences. If any row is under 1 sentence or over 3, revise before outputting.
- BATCH DEDUPLICATION: When generating multiple blog suggestions in one session, no two pieces may share the same hook sentence, CTA phrasing, or primary data source. Vary angle and evidence across every item in the batch. No two titles may share the same angle or target the same primary keyword.
- Output saved to output/Blog_Suggestions/

------------------------------------------------------------
FAILURE MODES TO AVOID
------------------------------------------------------------

1. TOPIC TOO BROAD / NOT A SPECIFIC ARTICLE ANGLE: "Corporate Training Trends" is a topic, not an angle. A viable suggestion names the specific reader problem and the specific lens the article takes to solve it.

2. DUPLICATE ANGLE ACROSS SUGGESTIONS: Generating 10 suggestions where 3 of them are variations of "how to measure training ROI" produces a low-value batch. Each suggestion must address a distinct reader problem.

3. KEYWORD-STUFFED TITLE: Titles that stack keywords without a clear benefit signal ("Corporate Training ROI Measurement Tools Guide 2026") perform poorly with both readers and search engines. Titles should read as something a human would click.

4. NO CLEAR READER BENEFIT IN THE ANGLE: An angle description that explains what the article covers without explaining why the reader should care fails the basic editorial test. Every angle description must answer "so what?" for the target audience.
