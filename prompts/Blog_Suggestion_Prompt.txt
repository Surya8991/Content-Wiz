============================================================
BLOG SUGGESTION PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Generate 10 blog topic ideas for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. The topics should be SEO-friendly, relevant to the niche "[NICHE/TOPIC AREA]", and suitable for publication on [PLATFORM e.g. Medium / WordPress / brand blog]. For each idea, provide a suggested title, a one-line description of the angle, and the primary target keyword. Do not use em dashes. Topics should range in difficulty and format (listicles, how-tos, guides, thought leadership).

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Suggested Title | Angle / Description | Primary Keyword | Format Type

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- 10 topic ideas per request
- Mix of formats: listicles, how-tos, ultimate guides, thought leadership, case studies
- Each title should be SEO-friendly with a natural keyword
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- Output saved to output/Blog_Suggestions/
