============================================================
BACKLINK OUTREACH EMAIL PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Write a backlink outreach email for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], to request a link from the target website "[TARGET WEBSITE NAME / URL]". The email should be addressed to the website editor or content team. Open with a genuine, specific compliment about their content. Introduce [BRAND NAME]'s resource at [RESOURCE URL] and explain clearly why it adds value to their existing content. Keep the ask natural and low-pressure. Write in a professional but personable tone. Do not use em dashes. Keep the email under 150 words.

Resource URL to promote: [INSERT URL]
Target Website / Page to get a link from: [INSERT TARGET URL]
Specific page on target site that relates to the resource (if known): [INSERT OR LEAVE BLANK]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Target Website | Resource URL | Subject Line | Email Body

Save to output/Backlink_Outreach/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Subject line must not sound like mass outreach
- Email under 150 words
- Must include a genuine specific reference to their content
- Ask must be natural, not pushy
- No em dashes anywhere
- Output saved to output/Backlink_Outreach/
- The identical subject line, opening compliment, or closing ask reused across multiple emails in the same batch - if you are generating more than one outreach email in a session, no two may share a subject line, opening line, or closing ask
