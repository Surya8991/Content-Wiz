============================================================
TESTIMONIAL / REVIEW REQUEST EMAIL PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Write a testimonial or review request email for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], to be sent to [RECIPIENT TYPE e.g. past training participants / corporate clients / course completers]. The email should thank the recipient for their engagement, reinforce value in 1–2 sentences (20–40 words), naming the specific outcome or transformation the recipient experienced. Example: 'The compliance training you completed last month cut your team's incident reports by 30% - that's a real result.' Avoid: 'We hope you enjoyed the training.' Then make a low-friction ask in 1 sentence (15–25 words): name the specific platform, estimate the time (e.g., '2 minutes'), and make the action clear. Example: 'Could you leave a 2-minute review on Google? It helps others make the same decision you did.' The ask is for either a written testimonial or a review on [PLATFORM e.g. Google / LinkedIn / Trustpilot / G2]. Include a direct link to the review page at [REVIEW URL]. Keep the tone genuine, appreciative, and conversational. Do not use em dashes. Avoid passive voice. Keep the email under 150 words.

Review Platform URL: [INSERT URL]
Recipient Type: [e.g. Corporate HR client / Individual course completer]
Voice/Tone: [warm and genuine / formal appreciation / conversational - default: genuine, warm, conversational]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Recipient Type | Review Platform | Subject Line | Email Body

Field encoding: wrap multi-line email bodies in double quotes.
Subject Line max 60 characters; must reference the specific product, programme, or experience - not a generic 'feedback request'.
Email Body must include: (1) genuine specific value reinforcement, (2) one low-friction ask, (3) review platform link or instruction.

Save to output/Testimonial_Request/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Subject line must feel personal, not automated
- Subject line max 60 characters
- Email under 150 words
- Ask must feel low-friction (one click to leave a review)
- Tone: warm, genuine, appreciative
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- SELF-CHECK: After drafting each email, count words. Target: under 150 words for the body. If over 150, cut the value-reinforcement paragraph to 1 sentence.
- Output saved to output/Testimonial_Request/
- The identical subject line or closing ask reused across multiple emails in the same batch - if you are generating more than one testimonial request in a session, no two may share a subject line or closing ask

------------------------------------------------------------
FAILURE MODES TO AVOID
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Value reinforcement that is generic and not tied to a specific outcome - 'We hope you enjoyed the training' fails; name a result the recipient experienced.
2. Email body over 150 words - trim to the core ask if needed.
3. Subject line over 60 characters - count before outputting.
4. Asking for a review and a referral in the same email - one ask per email only.
