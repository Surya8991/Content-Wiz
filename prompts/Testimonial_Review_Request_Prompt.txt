============================================================
TESTIMONIAL / REVIEW REQUEST EMAIL PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Write a testimonial or review request email for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], to be sent to [RECIPIENT TYPE e.g. past training participants / corporate clients / course completers]. The email should thank the recipient for their engagement, briefly reinforce the value they received, and make a warm, low-friction ask for either a written testimonial or a review on [PLATFORM e.g. Google / LinkedIn / Trustpilot / G2]. Include a direct link to the review page at [REVIEW URL]. Keep the tone genuine, appreciative, and conversational. Do not use em dashes. Keep the email under 150 words.

Review Platform URL: [INSERT URL]
Recipient Type: [e.g. Corporate HR client / Individual course completer]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Recipient Type | Review Platform | Subject Line | Email Body

Save to output/Testimonial_Request/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Subject line must feel personal, not automated
- Email under 150 words
- Ask must feel low-friction (one click to leave a review)
- Tone: warm, genuine, appreciative
- No em dashes anywhere
- Output saved to output/Testimonial_Request/
- The identical subject line or closing ask reused across multiple emails in the same batch - if you are generating more than one testimonial request in a session, no two may share a subject line or closing ask
