SKILLS GAP ANALYSIS REPORT PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Write a skills gap analysis report for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], for the industry or role "[INDUSTRY / ROLE / FUNCTION e.g. HR professionals in financial services / Project managers in tech companies]". The report should be 1200-1800 words and structured as follows: an executive summary, a market context section on why this skill gap matters now, identification of the top 5-8 skills gaps with evidence, the business impact of each gap, recommended training interventions for each gap, and a conclusion with a CTA linking to [URL]. Include at least 5 credible statistics or research references. Write in an authoritative, data-led tone targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE e.g. L&Ds, HRs, and decision-makers]. Do not use em dashes. Avoid passive voice. Naturally position [BRAND NAME] as the training partner that closes these gaps without being promotional.

Industry / Role Focus: [INSERT e.g. HR professionals / Sales teams / Project managers]
Reference URL: [INSERT URL]
Training Solutions to Reference (optional): [INSERT COURSE NAMES OR LEAVE BLANK]

------------------------------------------------------------
REPORT STRUCTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Report Title
2. Executive Summary (100-150 words)
3. Why This Skills Gap Matters Now (market context with data)
4. Skills Gap 1: Name | Evidence | Business Impact | Recommended Intervention
5. Skills Gap 2: Name | Evidence | Business Impact | Recommended Intervention
6. Skills Gap 3: Name | Evidence | Business Impact | Recommended Intervention
7. Skills Gap 4: Name | Evidence | Business Impact | Recommended Intervention
8. Skills Gap 5: Name | Evidence | Business Impact | Recommended Intervention
9. (Optional gaps 6-8)
10. Conclusion with CTA
11. About [BRAND NAME]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Skills Gap No. | Skill Name | Evidence / Stat | Business Impact | Recommended Training Intervention

Full report saved as plain text to output/Skills_Gap_Reports/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 5 skills gaps identified
- Each gap must include evidence, business impact, and a training recommendation
- Minimum 5 credible stats, each naming the source, organization, and year - never "studies show" or "research suggests" without attribution
- Brand positioned as training solution, not vendor pitch
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- Output saved to output/Skills_Gap_Reports/
