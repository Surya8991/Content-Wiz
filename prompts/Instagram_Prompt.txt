============================================================
INSTAGRAM POST PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Create an Instagram post caption for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], based on the blog post titled "[BLOG TITLE]". Open with a bold, attention-grabbing first line. Follow with 3-4 lines of value-driven insight targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Close with a call to action directing readers to the blog link in bio. Do not use em dashes. Add a line break followed by 8-10 relevant hashtags with the last hashtag always being [BRAND HASHTAG].

Blog URL: [INSERT BLOG URL]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Blog Title | Blog URL | Instagram Caption

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- First line must be a hook (bold statement, stat, or question)
- 3-4 insight lines after the hook
- CTA references "link in bio"
- 8-10 hashtags after a line break, last one is always the brand hashtag
- No em dashes anywhere
- Vary the exact phrasing of the hook and CTA post to post, do not reuse the same opening line or closing CTA sentence across a batch. Examples of the CTA pattern (write a new one each time, do not copy these verbatim): "Full breakdown linked in bio.", "Get the details at the link in bio.", "Read the whole story at the link in bio."
- The identical hook or CTA sentence reused across multiple posts in the same batch - if you are generating more than one Instagram post in a session, no two may share a hook or CTA
- Output saved to output/Instagram/
