============================================================
PRESS RELEASE PROMPT
============================================================

BRAND DETECTION: Follow _Brand_Detection_Rules.txt before generating.

Once the brand is confirmed, use the template below.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Write a press release for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], announcing "[ANNOUNCEMENT/NEWS TOPIC]". Follow standard press release format: headline, dateline, lead paragraph (who, what, when, where, why), body paragraphs with supporting detail, one executive quote attributed to the CEO of [BRAND NAME], and a boilerplate about [BRAND NAME] at the end. Target audience is [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Write in a formal, journalistic tone. If the release cites any statistic or figure, name its source, organization, and year, and never state an unverified or first-party number as fact without attribution. Do not use em dashes. Avoid passive voice. Keep the total length to 400-500 words.

URL: [INSERT URL IF APPLICABLE]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT
------------------------------------------------------------

Full press release in plain text with:
- FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE header
- Headline
- Dateline and lead paragraph
- Body (2-3 paragraphs)
- CEO quote
- Boilerplate
- Contact details placeholder

Save to output/Press_Release/

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Standard inverted pyramid structure
- CEO quote must feel authentic, not corporate
- Boilerplate is 3-4 sentences about the brand
- Any statistic or figure names its source, organization, and year, including first-party brand data; never present an unverified number as fact
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- 400-500 words total
- Output saved to output/Press_Release/
