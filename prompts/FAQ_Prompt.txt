------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPLATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Create a FAQ section for [BRAND NAME], [BRAND DESCRIPTION], on the topic "[TOPIC]". Generate 8-10 frequently asked questions with concise, authoritative answers targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Each answer should be 40-60 words, informative, and naturally aligned with [BRAND NAME]'s expertise. Include at least 2-3 answers that reference a credible stat or research finding. Do not use em dashes.

Blog URL (for reference): [INSERT URL IF APPLICABLE]

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT (CSV)
------------------------------------------------------------

Sr. No. | Topic | Blog URL | Question | Answer

------------------------------------------------------------
RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

- 8-10 Q&A pairs per topic
- Each answer 40-60 words
- At least 2-3 answers include a stat
- Every cited stat or research finding must name a source, organization, and year. Never invent a plausible-sounding citation
- No em dashes anywhere
- No passive voice
- Output saved to output/FAQ/
